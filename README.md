# OTT Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive analytics dashboard analyzing streaming content across major OTT platforms (Netflix, Hotstar, Prime Video).

## Overview

This dashboard provides insights into movies and series performance across different streaming platforms, with analysis of ratings, engagement, popularity, and trends.

## Key Metrics

- **Total Contents**: 2,500 movies and series
- **Average Rating**: 7.49/10
- **Average Engagement**: 1,071.84
- **Average Popularity**: 747.74
- **Trending Score**: 747.11
- **Total Votes**: 251,167,646

## Dashboard Features

### 1. **Platform Performance**
   - Compares key metrics across Netflix, Hotstar, and Prime Video
   - Shows average ratings, vote counts, popularity scores, and engagement metrics

### 2. **Movies vs Series**
   - Pie chart showing content distribution
   - Movies: 60% | Series: 40%

### 3. **Genre Popularity**
   - Thriller and Sci-Fi are the most popular genres
   - Data shown across Action, Comedy, Crime, Drama, Fantasy, and more

### 4. **Country-wise Content**
   - Content distribution across major countries
   - Includes USA, UK, Spain, South Korea, India, Germany, and others

### 5. **Top 10 Content**
   - Lists the most viewed/popular titles
   - Includes "Crimson Tide," "Cyber Night," "Silent Code," and more

### 6. **Year-wise Trend**
   - Shows content growth and engagement from 2015 to 2024
   - Tracks sum of ratings and votes over time

### 7. **Release Year Filter**
   - Data categorized by release year (2015-2024)

## Data Source

The dataset contains streaming content information including:
- Title and genre
- Platform availability
- User ratings and votes
- Popularity scores
- Release year
- Country of origin
